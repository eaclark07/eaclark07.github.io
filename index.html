About Me

I am a sixth year PhD student studying Natural Language Processing at the University of Washington. I am a student in the Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science & Engineering and a member of Noah Smith's ARK.

Currently, I'm working on natural language generation and evaluation, especially for human-computer collaborative story writing.
